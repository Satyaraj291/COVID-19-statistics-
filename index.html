About Our Work

During the times of the COVID-19 pandemic, the world has experienced very drastic changes. The pandemic has brought a complete change to the world whether it be the healing of the ozone layer or the downfall of the economy, we all have experienced the change in some way or others. But as the saying goes "when rich people fight the war with one another, poor people are the ones to die", and in the same manner in this fight between humans and the invisible opponent, they are the one who has suffered the greatest loss. We collected the data from the EOdashboard and few other sources to understand the problem these people are facing so that we can bring a systematical solution to these people.

Mobility Changes

Mobilty Reports In India

Source •COVID-19 Mobility Reports•EO Dashboard

To best way to know the effects of the covid-19 on the low-income and indigenous people is to visualize the mobility changes in different regions. According to the reports provided by eodashboard and google, the mobility trends in India have drastically changed. For example mobility in retails and recreation has decreased by 30% causing great loss to the small retailers and startup companies. Decrease or complete ban on the public transportation forced daily wage workers to walk to their home town which sometimes is miles away. Also due to the lack of any income source, these people live entirely on the donated food for months.
